**Financial Performance Analysis Dashboard**

Project Overview

This project analyzes financial performance across multiple regions and products using an interactive dashboard built in Power BI. The objective of the analysis is to evaluate revenue, profit, costs, and customer distribution to better understand business performance and identify key growth opportunities.

The dataset includes financial and operational data such as revenue, profit, marketing cost, operating cost, product categories, regions, and customer information. Through data visualization and business intelligence techniques, the dashboard provides insights into profitability, cost efficiency, and revenue trends.

**Dashboard Insights**

Financial Performance Summary

The first dashboard presents a high-level overview of the company’s financial performance. It highlights important metrics including total revenue, total profit, total customers, and profit margin.

The analysis reveals that the East region generates the highest profit, while Investment products generate the highest revenue among all product categories. Additionally, Insurance products have the highest profit margin, indicating stronger profitability compared to other products. The dashboard also shows that revenue has slightly increased over time, suggesting steady financial growth.

**Cost and Regional Analysis**

The second dashboard focuses on operational cost analysis and regional performance. It examines operating costs, marketing costs, customer distribution, and revenue patterns across regions.

The results indicate that the East region has the highest operating costs compared to other regions. The dashboard also highlights how customers are distributed across products and regions, helping identify areas where costs and customer engagement are highest.

**Business Questions Answered**

The dashboards were designed to answer the following key business questions:

Which region generates the highest profit?
Which product generates the highest revenue?
Which product has the highest profit margin?
Which region has the highest costs?
Is revenue increasing over time?

The insights generated from the analysis help support data-driven decision making related to product performance, regional profitability, and cost management.

**Tools Used**

Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard development
Data Modeling – KPI creation and aggregation
Business Intelligence Techniques – Financial performance analysis
